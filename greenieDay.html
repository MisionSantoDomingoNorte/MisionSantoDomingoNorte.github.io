🌱 Greenie Day Guide

Greenie Day happens once per transfer on the last day of the transfer—and it’s one of the most exciting parts of office life. You’ll meet all the new missionaries coming into the field, get them set up, and help kick off their mission on the right foot. Here's everything you need to know:

📱 1. Phones

✅ What to Expect:

  • Most greenies arrive with phones, but not all.
  • Bring a few backup phones to the meeting—just in case.
  • Any missionary from the DR who didn’t go to an in-person MTC will need a phone.

📋 What to Collect:

You’ll need to gather this info for every greenie phone:

  • Model
  • Model Number
  • Serial Number
  • IMEI

🕓 Do this on Greenie Day or by messaging them a day or two later—but don’t wait too long.

📽️ 2. The Slideshow Presentation

You’ll give a short tech intro as part of the day. Remember: greenies will be exhausted, so keep it fast, focused, and simple.

🔑 Key Points to Cover:

  • Phone password: 2nefi31 (make sure everyone uses this)
  • No Facebook except on P-Day
  • Complete Edify lessons to keep access to mission apps
  • Weekly phone audits during weekly planning with companions
  • Problems? → Tell them to contact you directly

💡 Pro Tip: Keep it light, maybe throw in a quick laugh or two. They're nervous—help them feel at home.

🌳 3. The Park Experience

If you’re asked to participate, do it! It’s one of the most memorable moments.

  • You’ll be assigned a temporary comp.
  • Head out to the park next to the temple and help the greenies make their first contacts.
  • Make sure all your office work is done beforehand, or you won’t have time to catch up later.

📸 4. The Wrap-Up

After the park:

  1. Group photo in front of the temple.
  2. Return for your presentation.
  3. Eat and hang out a bit.
  4. 🎉 That’s it—Greenie Day complete.
