REFERENCES

Learn how to handle references in the mission.

Step 1

Open the Referral Manager.

Step 2

Open the extension and copy the data.

Step 3

Open the Google Sheet and ensure that the user ('usuario') is correct. You should only need to change this the first time you do this. Then, update the Token by clicking the box and pressing Ctrl + V. Click Update and wait for the sheet to fully refresh. Once updated, take a screenshot of the table and send it to the mission.

Step 4

Return to the Referral Manager page and click the light blue box under "Action Items."

Step 5

You will now see all unassigned referrals. Select one and prepare to send it.

Step 6

After opening a referral, check the timeline for any previous actions. Sometimes referrals were sent previously but lost in the system. If no recent notes exist, you can either resend the referral to the missionary or assign it to another area. In this example, there are no issues, so we proceed by clicking the underlined icon.

Step 7

Follow these steps to assign the referral correctly:

  • 1. Select the bar to choose a specific area within the mission.
  • 2. Review the address provided. If valid, you can locate it on the map.
  • 3. Select to prevent accidental address changes when interacting with the map.
  • 4. Click Send to assign the referral.

Step 8

If the referral lacks identifiable information, assign it to the area with the fewest active referrals. Open Google Sheets and find the "Send Here" section.

Referral Distribution Graph Explanation:

  • Red: Referrals with no attempted contact.
  • Green: Referrals with at least one contact attempt.
  • White number: Total referrals in the area.
